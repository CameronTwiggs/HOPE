What is oppression and what are we up against? Oppression in its purest form is the prevention of growth against individuals or groups of people. The oppression we are battling against, however, is the oppression led by those with political power. The very same people meant to express our thoughts and ideas in the political world, are now working against us. This is an oppression that must change, and it must change now..

How can you make a change in your community? With our website, you can easily access the necessary information to know our political figures. Having quick access to this knowledge, you can know who is the person responsible without having to go through an extensive process to find who is in command.

KNOW YOUR RIGHTS

The goal of our non-profit organization is to educate others about how to access the information of those in power, this is to understand who is the root cause for the oppression in our communities. To know who represents you and your community is essential to the progression you wish to make, not just community wide, but perhaps even nationwide. Changing those who represent you and your community is the first and most important step in working towards a future you want.

The governor is the collected embodiment of the people in the state. They are the ones who speak for you, so make sure you get a person in the position you want to voice your ideas.
